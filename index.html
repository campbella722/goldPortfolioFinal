About me


My name is Arianne. I am currently enrolled in the Eleven Fifty Academy's Web Development Part-Time Bootcamp program. Concurrently, I am also a high school counselor for an online public charter school in Indiana. I have been serving in this position since 2016, shortly after earning my Master of Science in Education degree from Purdue University at the Calumet campus in Hammond, IN in 2015. My interest in web development and coding began as a result of my exposure to information technology career exploration as part of my duties as a course advisor to students specifically pursuing this career cluster.


A little about me, I was born and raised in Gary, IN, on the southern tip of Lake Michigan, about 30 minutes outside Chicago, IL, and currently reside in the Northwest Indiana region. In addition to earning my master's degree, I have earned my Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology from Purdue University in West Lafayette, IN. I am also an alumna of the Indiana Academy for Science, Mathematics, and Humanities, a gifted and talented college preparatory high scool on Ball State campus in Muncie, IN. My interests include cooking, karaoke, gardening, board games, sailing, sewing, hiking, and watching movies. When possible, I enjoy travelling to new places and exploring the outdoors with my husband, Matt. We are both very involved with a local professional wrestling company in our area. In addition to holding the PRW Ladies' Championship, I have been responsible for the creation of previous marketing materials such as flyers as well as video editing episodes for upload to our YouTube Channel. You can find us now on the PRW channel on Roku and InfiniStream.
